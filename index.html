Covid-19 transmission: RMCH the new epicenter

Star on The Spot


Rajshahi Medical College Hospital (RMCH) has become the new epicenter of Covid-19 infection as regular and Covid-19 patients are being treated under the same roof.

For all latest news, follow The Daily Star's Google News channel.

As each day passes, the Covid-19 situation in Rajshahi is getting worse -- with daily detection rate fluctuating between 30 to over 40 percent, and patient influx higher than ever.

The Daily Star correspondent Anwar Ali went to witness the situation in RMCH. Watch his experience in Star on The Spot.
